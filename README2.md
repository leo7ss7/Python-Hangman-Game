# This is a simple Hang-Man game 😵🔤

### How does it work?
Its designed for two players:
One chooses the word, while the other tries to guess it
To play it, you can simply run the program and the game will start
in your terminal

### How to play it?
Once player 1 picked a word, player 2 can choose between
guessing a letter that may be in the word, or, if he feels cofident,
try to guess the entire word

If you fail, the stickman loses a limb each time, until only the head
is left. Be careful ;)

### When does it end?
The player wins once he guesses all letters in the word,
or the entire word itself
The player loses if he fails 7 times
